# Rock-Paper-Scissor
This is a simple web-based Rock-Paper-Scissors game called "First-Five". The game pits you against a bot in a race to be the first to score five points. It's a classic game of chance and strategy.

## How to Play
Click on one of the three buttons (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) to make your choice.
The bot will randomly select its choice.
The winner of each round is determined by the classic rules: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
The first player to reach a score of five wins the game.

## Features
Visual feedback with images representing your and the bot's choices.
Real-time score tracking for both you and the bot.
Clear indication of the winner after each round.
Option to play again once the game is over.
